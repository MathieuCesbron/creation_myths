the great white goose tengri (tengri ülgen) flew over the primordial waters (time). at the urging of the white mother below he began to create. first, in his loneliness, he created er kishi, a devil-like figure who supposedly would help the creator. er kishi undermined the creation, however, and tengri ülgen left to remain in heaven, where from he sent sacred animals to guide the people he had created. shamans made their way to the fifth heaven to consult with the divine spirits.
